Green Bank Credit Card Agreement

Section 1: Interest Rates and Interest Charges
Clause 1.1: Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for Purchases
Your APR for purchases is 24.99% variable, depending on the Prime Rate. Interest charges will begin to accrue from the transaction date.

Clause 1.2: Penalty APR
If you fail to make your minimum payment by the due date for two consecutive billing cycles, a Penalty APR of 29.99% may be applied to your entire balance. This Penalty APR will remain indefinitely.

Section 2: Fees and Penalties
Clause 2.1: Late Payment Fee
A late payment fee of up to $40 will be charged if the minimum payment is not received by 5:00 PM Eastern Time on the due date.

Clause 2.2: Foreign Transaction Fees
A 3% fee applies to every transaction made in a foreign currency or outside of the United States.

Section 3: Account Closure
Clause 3.1: Inactivity
Green Bank reserves the right to close your credit card account without prior notice if there is no activity for 12 consecutive months.
