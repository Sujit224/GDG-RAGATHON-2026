Sunset Apartments Rental Contract

Section 1: Rent and Deposits
Clause 1.1: Rent Payments
Rent of $1,800 is due on the 1st of every month. Payments received after the 5th of the month are subject to a late fee of $100 plus $10 for every additional day late.

Clause 1.2: Security Deposit 
A security deposit of $1,800 is required prior to move-in. The deposit will be returned within 30 days of lease termination, minus any deductions for cleaning or damages beyond normal wear and tear.

Section 2: Rules and Restrictions
Clause 2.1: Pet Policy
Pets are only allowed with written approval from management. Approved pets require a non-refundable $300 pet fee and an additional $50 per month in pet rent. Unauthorized pets will incur a penalty of $500.

Clause 2.2: Alterations
Tenants may not make any permanent alterations to the property, including painting, changing locks, or installing heavy fixtures, without written permission.

Section 3: Termination
Clause 3.1: Early Lease Termination
If a tenant breaks the lease before the standard 12-month term ends, they are responsible for an early termination fee equivalent to two months' rent ($3,600).
