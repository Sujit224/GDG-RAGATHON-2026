Section I: Definitions


Clause 1.1: Emergency Medical Condition – A medical condition with acute symptoms (including severe pain) where a prudent layperson would expect that skipping immediate medical help would seriously jeopardize their health.


Clause 1.2: Network Provider – A hospital, clinic, or physician with a direct contract with Titan Secure to provide services at negotiated rates.


Clause 1.3: Deductible – An initial amount of $500.00 USD that the Insured must pay out-of-pocket before reimbursement begins.

Section II: Scope of Coverage


Clause 2.1: Inpatient Hospitalization – Covers room, board, nursing, and ICU charges up to $150,000 per year, provided the stay exceeds 24 consecutive hours.

Clause 2.2: Outpatient Services & Diagnostics


Laboratory Tests: Covered at 90% after the deductible.

Imaging (MRI/CT): Requires prior authorization from the Titan Medical Board (TMB). Failure to get this results in a 40% penalty on the total claim.

Prescription Drugs: Tier 1 (Generic) is 100% covered; Tier 2 (Brand Name) is 50% covered.

Section III: Specific Exclusions (The "Fine Print")


Clause 3.1: Cosmetic Surgery – Procedures for aesthetic improvement rather than restoration of function are not covered.


Clause 3.2: Extreme Sports Injuries – Injuries from high-risk activities like Base Jumping, Skydiving, Cave Diving, and Professional MMA are not covered.


Clause 3.3: Pre-existing Conditions (The 24-Month Rule) – Conditions treated within 24 months before the policy start date won't be covered until the policy has been active for 730 continuous days.


Clause 3.4: Alternative Medicine – Acupuncture, Homeopathy, and Ayurveda are excluded (not included in the Platinum tier).

Section IV: Claims Procedures


Clause 4.1: Notification – Non-emergency claims must be filed within 30 days of the medical event.


Clause 4.2: Documentation – Claims require original invoices and a signed "Physician's Statement of Necessity".


Clause 4.3: Dispute Resolution – Disagreements are settled via binding arbitration in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh.

Section V: Renewal & Cancellation


Clause 5.1: Grace Period – A 15-day period is granted for premium payments after the first one.


Clause 5.2: Early Termination – If cancelled within the first 180 days, a $75.00 "Pro-Rata Administrative Fee" is deducted from the refund.
